## PART 1: THE PROBLEM

### Why Enterprises Fail at Lean and Six Sigma

Every enterprise claims Lean.  
Every enterprise claims Six Sigma.  
Almost none actually achieve them.

**Why?**

Because Lean and Six Sigma assume:
- Clean data
- Stable processes
- Controlled variation
- Consistent operators
- Traceable decisions

**Those assumptions never hold in modern, multi-system enterprises.**

### The Reality: Firefighting on Poor Data

The cycle is always the same:

```
Poor Data ? Firefighting ? Worse Data ? More Firefighting ? Permanent Crisis Mode
```

**What actually happens:**

- Data scattered across 7+ disconnected systems
- Logs are missing, incomplete, or untrustworthy
- Processes drift silently without detection
- Operators improvise because systems don't match reality
- Nobody trusts the numbers, so decisions are political
- Improvement initiatives rot in SharePoint after 90 days
- Six Sigma Black Belts become consultants instead of fixing processes
- Lean value streams exist only in PowerPoint

**The result:**

Lean tools measure waste that doesn't exist while missing waste that does.  
Six Sigma analyzes variation in data that's fundamentally unreliable.  
Root cause analysis becomes educated guesswork.  
Compliance becomes performance theater.

### The Real Cost

**Boeing 737 MAX:** $20B+ in losses, 346 deaths — inability to prove software behavior.  
**Volkswagen Dieselgate:** €30B in fines and settlements — software governance failure.  
**British Post Office Horizon:** £1B+ in compensation, wrongful convictions — system integrity not provable.  
**Every major GDPR fine:** Organization could not prove how they handled personal data.

**The pattern:** *When you cannot prove system behavior, catastrophic failure becomes a legal and financial certainty.*

### Why "Digital Transformation" Doesn't Fix It

Most digital transformation initiatives:
- Add more dashboards (which lie differently)
- Integrate systems (creating new failure modes)
- Deploy AI (which inherits the data problems)
- Automate processes (locking in the chaos)

**They don't fix the root problem:** *The system cannot tell you what it actually did, or prove it.*
